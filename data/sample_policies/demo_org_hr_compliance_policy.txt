ACME Corp HR Compliance Policy v1.0

Section 2.1 - Working Hours Limit
Employees working more than 48 hours per week must trigger a compliance warning
and require Line Manager review before timesheet submission is accepted.

Section 2.2 - Overtime Controls
Overtime hours above 48 hours per week must not be approved without manager review.

Section 3.1 - Audit Requirements
Every compliance warning and manager review event must be logged with reviewer
identity and timestamp.

Section 4.1 - Agent Automation Limits
No autonomous system changes to HR compliance or working-hours workflows are permitted.
All changes require human approval before merge to production.

Section 5.1 - Required Sign-off Roles
- HR Manager
- Line Manager
- Compliance Officer
